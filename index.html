No-show Flagging | Gushyira Akamenyetso | Signalement Absence

Staff can flag customers who don't pick up their order. After 2 flags, customer deposit increases from 500 RWF to 1000 RWF automatically.

Abakozi bashobora gushyira akamenyetso ku bakiriya bataza gufata komande. Nyuma y'ibendera 2, inguzanyo y'umukiriya yiyongera kuva RWF 500 kugera RWF 1000 by'ubwayo.
